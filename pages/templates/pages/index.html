Letter from the Secretary-General

Honorable Delegates, Advisors, and Sponsors,

My name is Michael Almond and I am currently a 4th-year student at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville. This year, I have the honor of serving as the Secretary-General for our annual high school conference, Volunteer Model United Nations (VolMUN). We are incredibly excited to host the 5th iteration of our conference in Knoxville from February 7-9, 2020!

Although VolMUN IV ended not too long ago, our staff has already begun drafting ideas in preparation for VolMUN V! As Secretary-General, my goal is to ever improve the experience for all guests of the conference. Additionally, I will also strive to maintain the high level of integrity that VolMUN has built as a college-preparatory conference.

Ever since VolMUN IV ended, my Secretariat and I have been working to create a memorable experience for all attendees of the conference. Our goals include improving upon our past conferences and maintaining VolMUN’s high level of integrity as a college-preparatory conference.

In order to keep with our mission of striving to be a premier Southeastern high school Model UN conference, we have made some new additions to the conference that I am excited to share with you! These include:

  • Hosting VolMUN V in the brand new Student Union, allowing for all components of the conference to take place in one facility;
  • Switching registration systems to a 3rd party platform known as “MUN Manager’ in order to streamline the registration and delegate upload processes;
  • Updating our conference schedule to implement a VolMUN Workshop preceding the Opening Ceremonies (to teach delegates the skills needed to succeed in committee) and a dedicated campus tour/Q&A Session for prospective students to the University of Tennessee;
  • Fully revamping our website (www.volmun.com) in order to make the website more user-friendly and aesthetically-pleasing;
  • Creating a new Instagram page for VolMUN (@volmunutk) in order to provide more updates on the status of the conference;
  • Implementing a new team within the VolMUN staff known as the Digital Press Team, whose goal is to more efficiently and creatively document the progress of the conference.

Although registration for the conference will not open for another month, I hope that you will begin getting your Model UN club excited for the conference in February! In the meantime, feel free to check out our website full of documents from last year’s conference as well as our promotional video for VolMUN V, and follow our new Instagram page (@volmunutk)! Additionally, if you have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out!

Thank you for your continued support of VolMUN, and I hope to be in contact with you soon!

Regards,

Michael Almond
Secretary-General of VolMUN V
